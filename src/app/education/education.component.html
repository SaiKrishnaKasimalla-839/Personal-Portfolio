Schooling is a formative period in a person's life where they undergo structured education to develop academic, social, and personal skills. It typically begins in early childhood with preschool or kindergarten and extends through primary, secondary, and sometimes tertiary education. During schooling, students engage in various subjects such as mathematics, science, language arts, and social studies, while also participating in extracurricular activities like sports, arts, and clubs. It also fosters friendships, encourages teamwork, and lays the foundation for lifelong learning.
